About this application

This is a prototype visualization app for the Cerro Cantil protected area in Guatemala. The purpose of the app is to offer central access to several Earth-observation datasets to monitor the health of the protected area. Initially, the application incorporates fire monitoring (from FIRMS), both historical and current, and high-resolution imagery made available under research grants to SERVIR.

Status: Active @ SCO
